## The methodological puzzle

I'm stuck on how to handle the measurement invariance question. The short version: my main comparison assumes that the latent construct is measured equivalently across groups, but I haven't formally tested this. Running a full configural → metric → scalar invariance sequence would be the right move, but it complicates the results section significantly.

Options:
1. Run the tests, report them in the appendix, proceed if metric invariance holds.
2. Acknowledge the assumption as a limitation without testing it.
3. Reframe the comparison to make a weaker claim that doesn't require full scalar invariance.

Option 1 is the most defensible. Option 3 is potentially more interesting but requires more thinking.
